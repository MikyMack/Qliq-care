About Qliq Care

Your Trusted Home Healthcare Partner

Empowering homes with professional, world-class & affordable home healthcare, because we care for you!

At Qliq care, we deeply understand the challenges many face post-surgery or when battling chronic pain. The post-operative phase or the recuperating period, especially for the elderly, demands unwavering attention and utmost care. But in the hustle and bustle of today’s life, it’s not always feasible to be by their side throughout. That’s where we step in.
